Choose Your Plan

X Premium is an opt-in, paid subscription that offers additional features to improve your experience. X Premium has three tiers: Basic, Premium, and Premium+, with more features available in each higher tier.

Basic

$3/mo

Includes all essential features like:

  • editing posts
  • longer posts & video uploads
  • reply prioritization
  • custom app icons

Premium

$8/mo

Includes all Basic features plus:

  • a checkmark
  • reduced ads
  • access to ad revenue sharing
  • larger reply prioritization

Premium+

$16/mo

Includes all Premium features plus:

  • no ads in the For You timeline
  • no ads in the Following timeline
  • largest reply prioritization
  • access to Grok AI chatbot
